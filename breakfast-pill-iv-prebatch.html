Breakfast Pill IV Prebatched Base

The clarified base for the Breakfast Pill IV cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 3 parts Bacon Fat Washed Bear Face Rye
  • 1 part Maple & Brown Sugar Syrup
  • 20 Dashes Angostura Bitters
  • 20 Dashes Orange Bitters
  • 8 Drops Saline Solution (20%)
  • 8 Drops MSG Solution (2%)
  • 2 Large Egg Whites

Prep for Bacon Fat Washed Bear Face Rye:

  • Combine 5 parts Bear Face Rye with 1 part rendered bacon fat (cooled but still liquid).
  • Let it sit at room temperature for 4 hours.
  • Freeze overnight, then strain the fat.

Prep for Maple & Brown Sugar Syrup:

  • Combine equal parts rich brown sugar syrup and pure maple syrup.

Preparation

  1. Combine Base Ingredients: In a large mixing container, combine the prepared Bacon Fat Washed Bear Face Rye, Maple & Brown Sugar Syrup, Angostura Bitters, Orange Bitters, Saline Solution, and MSG Solution. Stir thoroughly until fully integrated.
  2. Prepare Egg Whites: In a separate bowl, lightly whisk the 2 large egg whites until they are just frothy.
  3. Integrate: Slowly pour the cocktail base mixture into the frothy egg whites, whisking gently.
  4. Chill & Rest: Cover and refrigerate for at least 8-12 hours, or preferably overnight.
  5. Fine Filtration: Set up your fine filtration system with a funnel and cheesecloth or a Superbag.
  6. Slow Pour: Very slowly and carefully pour the chilled mixture into your prepared filter. Avoid disturbing the sediment.
  7. Re-filter (If Needed): If the first pass isn't perfectly clear, gently re-filter the liquid through a clean filter.
  8. Store the clarified base in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
