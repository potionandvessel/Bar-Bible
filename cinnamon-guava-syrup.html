Cinnamon-Guava Syrup

A spiced tropical syrup that adds a warm and fruity base to cocktails.

Ingredients (by Parts)

  • 2 parts Guava Purée
  • 1 part Sugar
  • 1 part Water
  • Cinnamon Sticks to taste
  • Ground Cinnamon to taste

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Whisk gently until the sugar and guava purée have fully dissolved.
  3. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes to an hour.
  4. Taste the syrup periodically and stop simmering once the cinnamon flavor has fully incorporated.
  5. Let the syrup cool completely.
  6. Strain the syrup to remove the cinnamon sticks.
  7. Transfer to a sealed storage container.
  8. Label, date, and initial.
  9. Store in the refrigerator for up to 1 month.

Tips

Rinse the cinnamon sticks after use to empty out the hollow bits of syrup, as a lot of flavor gets trapped in there.
