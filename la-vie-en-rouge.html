xx

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ oz. Vodka
  • ½ oz. Licor 43
  • ¾ oz. Raspberry Puree
  • ½ oz. Pseudo Lemon Juice
  • ½ oz. Cranberry Juice
  • 3 Dashes Aquafaba
  • 1 Drop Saline Solution

Preparation

  1. Add ingredients into a mixing glass.
  2. Dry Shake / Wet Shake.
  3. Strain into a chilled Nick & Nora Glass.
  4. Make a heart design with Peychaud's bitters.
  5. Garnish with a raspberry.
  6. Spritz with Lemon.
  7. Serve.
