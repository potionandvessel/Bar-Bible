The Magic Marg

A cocktail so perfectly balanced, it seems to have been discovered in every reality.

My relationship with the Margarita is about honoring the agave. That's what makes both tequila and mezcal truly sing. I like to use a blend of both 100% pure spirits, along with a pure sweetener, to bring out their lovely complexity. My favorite spin is the swap to Gran Marnier; its brandy base adds a wonderful depth to the orange slot. And why not boost it with a little fresh-squeezed orange and lime? Freshness is key here. Ultimately, isn't a Margarita just a tequila sour at its heart? This version aims for a smooth, velvety consistency, not a thick foam, to highlight that truth.

Ingredients

  • 1¾ oz. Reposado Tequila
  • ¼ oz. Mezcal
  • ½ oz. Gran Marnier
  • 1 oz. Fresh Lime Juice
  • ¼ oz. Orange Juice
  • ½ oz. Agave Syrup
  • 3 Dashes Aquafaba
  • 1 Drop Saline Solution

Preparation

  1. Add all ingredients to a mixing glass.
  2. Shake until chilled.
  3. Rim a rocks glass with half Tajin and half sea salt.
  4. Strain over cubed ice into the prepared glass.
  5. Garnish with a dehydrated lime wheel.
  6. Spritz with lime.
  7. Serve.
