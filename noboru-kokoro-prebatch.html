Noboru Kokoro Pre-Batch

A new essence born from the masterful clarification of a classic form.

Ingredients

Combine the following in parts to create your pre-batch:

  • 8 parts Suntori Japanese Whisky
  • 4 parts Yuzu Juice
  • 3 parts Simple Syrup

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients.
  2. Add one part homogenized whole milk for every five parts of the pre-batch liquid.****
  3. Stir briefly and allow to curdle.
  4. Strain through a cheesecloth or coffee filter until clear.
  5. Store in a sealed bottle in the refrigerator.

**** The technique of milk clarification will be detailed in a separate 'Techniques' page soon.
