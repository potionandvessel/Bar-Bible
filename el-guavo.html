Ingredients

  • 2 oz. El Guavo Pre Batch
  • 1 oz. Cinnamon-Guava Syrup
  • 1 oz. Pseudo Lime Juice
  • 4 Japanese Dashes Tobacco Bitters
  • 1 Drop Saline Solution

Preparation

  1. Add ingredients into a mixing glass.
  2. Shake till cold.
  3. Strain onto cubed ice in a rocks glass.
  4. Garnish with dehydrated lime and cinnamon cigar.
  5. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
  6. Spritz with Lime.
  7. Serve.
