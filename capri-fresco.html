Modern / Umami / Clarified / Culinary-Inspired

Inspired by the sun-drenched Italian island and its iconic salad, "Capri Fresco" is a breathtaking reinterpretation of the classic Caprese. This cocktail captures the fresh essence of ripe tomatoes, aromatic basil, and delicate mozzarella in an unexpected and elegant way. Each sip offers a harmonious blend of savory depth, bright herbaceous notes, and a subtle richness, culminating in an incredibly smooth and unforgettable finish.

Ingredients

  • 4 oz. Capri Fresco Prebatch
  • Basil Oil

Preparation

  1. Rim half a rocks glass with Capri Rim
  2. In a Yari, stir prebatch over ice for 15 seconds.
  3. Strain over a king cube in your rimmed glass.
  4. Drop 3 drops of basil oil on top of the drink, around the cube in a diamond shape, excluding the side without rim.
  5. Place a Caprese Skewer on the rim of the glass on the side without oil or rim.
  6. Serve.

Tips

Lots of prep work is involved. Be sure to be on top of stock of; Capri Vodka, Basil Oil, Caprese Skewers, Capri Rim, Clarified Pre Batch & King Cubes. Oil is heavy in the eye dropper, make sure not to put more than needed.
