Noboru Kokoro

A cocktail with a powerful double entendre, meaning “lifting spirits.”

This cocktail was inspired by Truffle on the Rocks and a more classic variation he taught me. That recipe showed me how to clarify and was a delicious cocktail I was able to alter to put on the menu at a Japanese-owned restaurant. This drink is a true testament to the idea that some cocktails are so good, they transcend space and time to pop up in different places—even The Blue Crab.

Ingredients

For a single serving, you will need:

  • 4 oz. Noboru Kokoro Pre-Batch
  • 1 Drop Saline Solution
  • ½ oz. Pinot Noir

Preparation

  1. In a mixing glass, briefly stir the pre-batch and saline together.
  2. Pour over a king cube in a rocks glass.
  3. Using the back of a bar spoon, carefully float the Pinot Noir on top.
  4. Spritz with lemon.
  5. Serve.

Tips

Use the back of a bar spoon to float the wine on top and create a perfect layer. A little Pinot Noir goes a long way to give this drink a beautiful color and a dry, tannic finish.
