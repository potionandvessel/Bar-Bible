"Matcha Fragola" is a vibrant and inviting cocktail designed with a sophisticated, approachable, and distinctly feminine appeal. Inspired by the delicate balance of Japanese matcha and the universally loved pairing of strawberries and cream, it evokes a sense of luxurious indulgence. This drink is a statement of elegance and playful allure, making every sip a moment of delightful discovery.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ oz. Vodka
  • ½ oz. Galliano
  • ½ oz. Fresh Lemon Juice
  • ⅝ oz. Strawberry Syrup
  • 1 Teaspoon Sifted Matcha Powder
  • 1 Dash Aquafaba

Preparation

  1. Sift matcha into a shaker tin.
  2. Add remaining ingredients.
  3. Wet Shake // Dry Shake
  4. Strain into a chilled coupe glass.
  5. Garnish with a dehydrated strawberry.
  6. Serve.

Tips

Dry shake with a single ice cube to ensure a good seal is created. Leave ample prep time to produce the dehydrated strawberries and strawberry syrup, and keep a nice full back stock.
