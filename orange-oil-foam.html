Orange Oil Foam

Ingredients

  • Peels from 3-4 large oranges
  • 1/2 cup Superfine Sugar
  • 4 oz Arancello
  • Approx. 8-10 oz Water
  • 0.5g - 0.7g Soy Lecithin Powder
  • 0.1g - 0.15g Xanthan Gum

Preparation

  1. Oleo-Saccharum: In a bowl, muddle the orange peels and sugar vigorously until the oils are extracted. Let the mixture sit for 2-4 hours, then strain and discard the peels.
  2. Foam Liquid: Combine the Oleo-Saccharum, Arancello, and water. Add the Soy Lecithin Powder and Xanthan Gum, and blend with an immersion blender until smooth.
  3. Charge & Chill: Pour the foam liquid into a whipped cream dispenser. Charge with one N₂O charger, shake, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before use.
