Breakfast Pill IV

Ingredients

  • Breakfast Pill IV Prebatched Base
  • Orange Oil Foam

Preparation

  1. Pour 3 oz. of the prebatched base into a yari.
  2. Stir with ice until chilled.
  3. Strain over a king cube in an Old Fashioned Glass.
  4. Dispense a generous dollop of Orange Oil Foam on top.
  5. Garnish with a dehydrated orange half wheel.
