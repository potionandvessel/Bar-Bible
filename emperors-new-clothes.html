Ingredients

  • 1 ½ oz. Empress Gin
  • ½ oz. Soho
  • 1 ½ oz. Grapefruit Juice
  • ½ Lemon Juice
  • 1/4 oz. Simple Syrup
  • Tonic
  • 1 Drop Saline Solution

Preparation

  1. Add ingredients except gin and tonic.
  2. Shake till cold.
  3. Strain onto cubed ice in a Collins Glass.
  4. Add a splash of tonic leaving room for gin.
  5. Float gin on top.
  6. Garnish with a lemon twist.
  7. Spritz with lemon.
  8. Serve.

Tips

Pour the gin slowly so as to not mix too much. Let the guest know to stir the drink at some point to incorporate the gin.
