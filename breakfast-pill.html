Breakfast Pill IV

Yields: Approx. 8-10 servings

Prep Time: 15 mins active, 8-12 hours chill/clarification time for base, 2-4 hours for oleo-saccharum, 30 mins chill for foam.

Part 1: Clarified Cocktail Base

Ingredients for the Batch (approx. 24 oz liquid for clarification):

  • Bacon Fat Washed Bear Face Rye: 16 oz
  • Maple & Brown Sugar Syrup: 5 oz
  • Arancello: 2.5 oz
  • Angostura Bitters: 40 Dashes
  • Saline Solution (20%): 8 Drops
  • MSG Solution (2%): 8 Drops
  • Large Egg Whites: 2

Clarification Equipment:

  • Large mixing bowl or container
  • Whisk
  • Airtight container
  • Fine-mesh strainer
  • Cheesecloth (4-6 layers) OR large coffee filters OR large Superbag/Nut Milk Bag
  • Funnel
  • Clean receiving container

Clarification Method:

  1. Combine Base Ingredients: In a large mixing container, combine the Rye, Maple & Brown Sugar Syrup, Arancello, Angostura Bitters, Saline Solution, and MSG Solution. Stir thoroughly until fully integrated.
  2. Prepare Egg Whites: In a separate bowl, lightly whisk the 2 large egg whites until they are just frothy.
  3. Integrate: Slowly pour the cocktail base mixture into the frothy egg whites, whisking gently.
  4. Chill & Rest: Cover and refrigerate for at least 8-12 hours, or preferably overnight.
  5. Fine Filtration: Set up your fine filtration system with a funnel and cheesecloth.
  6. Slow Pour: Very slowly and carefully pour the chilled mixture into your prepared filter.
  7. Re-filter (If Needed): If the first pass isn't perfectly clear, gently re-filter the liquid. Store the clarified base in an airtight container in the refrigerator.

Part 2: Orange Oil Foam

Ingredients for the Foam:

  • Peels from 3-4 large oranges
  • 1/2 cup Superfine Sugar
  • 4 oz Arancello
  • Approx. 8-10 oz Water
  • 0.5g - 0.7g Soy Lecithin Powder
  • 0.1g - 0.15g Xanthan Gum

Foam Equipment:

  • Mixing bowl
  • Muddler
  • Precision scale
  • Immersion blender or whisk
  • 500ml (or 1L) whipped cream dispenser (iSi siphon)
  • Nitrous Oxide (N₂O) chargers

Foam Method:

  1. Make Oleo-Saccharum: Place orange peels and sugar in a bowl. Muddle vigorously until oils are extracted. Let sit for 2-4 hours. Strain and discard peels.
  2. Prepare Foam Liquid: Combine oleo-saccharum, Arancello, and water. Add the Soy Lecithin Powder and Xanthan Gum. Use an immersion blender to blend until smooth.
  3. Charge & Chill: Pour the foam liquid into your whipped cream dispenser. Charge with one N₂O charger. Shake and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Part 3: Final Assembly (Per Serving)

  • Glassware: Old Fashioned Glass
  • Ice: King Cube
  • Garnish: Orange Oil Foam

Assembly Method:

  1. Place one King Cube in an Old Fashioned Glass.
  2. Pour 2 oz of the clarified "Breakfast Pill IV" base over the ice.
  3. Gently shake the whipped cream dispenser a few times.
  4. Invert the dispenser and carefully dispense a generous dollop of Orange Oil Foam on top of the cocktail.
